Links between Cultural Heritage and Human Rights

Several links can be established between cultural heritage and human rights:

  • Cultural rights are human rights.
  • The protection of cultural rights may indirectly protect human rights more widely and more generally.
  • Cultural Heritage may, however, also be in conflict with, and even threaten, human rights.
  • There may be a parallel on the level of norm enforcement: individual criminal liability may be incurred for the violation of both human rights and cultural property or heritage.
